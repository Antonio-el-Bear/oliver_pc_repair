Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most software repairs and upgrades are completed within 24 hours. Hardware repairs may take 1-3 days depending on parts availability. We always provide an estimated completion time upfront.

Do you offer pickup and delivery service?

Yes! We offer free pickup and delivery within Pretoria for all repair services. Just give us a call to schedule a convenient time.

Is diagnostic service really free?

Absolutely! We offer completely free diagnostics to identify the problem with your computer. You only pay if you decide to proceed with the repair.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers (EFT), and all major credit/debit cards. Payment is required upon completion of the service.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 30-day warranty on labor. Parts warranties vary by manufacturer but are typically covered for 3-12 months.

Can you recover data from a dead hard drive?

In most cases, yes! We have specialized tools and techniques for data recovery. We'll perform a free assessment to determine if your data is recoverable before proceeding.
